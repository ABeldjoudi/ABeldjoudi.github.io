Privacy Policy for WalkWithUs
Effective Date: February 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how WalkWithUs ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your personal information when you use our mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Collect
We collect the following personal information directly from you when you register or use the App:

Name
Email address
Age
City and neighborhood
Location data (GPS) when you use the Walk Tracking feature
Step count and motion data (when available on your device)
We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18 years old.

2. Location Data and Walk Tracking
When you use the Walk Tracking feature, we collect your GPS location to:

Track your walking route in real-time
Calculate distance, steps, and calories burned
Display your route on a map
Your detailed walking routes are stored locally on your device only. We only collect aggregate statistics (total distance, steps, calories) to provide you with monthly trends and to generate anonymous community statistics.

You can disable location tracking at any time through your device settings. The Walk Tracking feature is entirely optional.

3. How We Use Your Information
We use your personal information to:

Create and manage your user account
Allow you to organize or join group walks
Track your personal walking statistics (when you choose to use this feature)
Communicate with you about walks, updates, or support requests
Improve the functionality and user experience of the App
Ensure basic safety, coordination, and accountability between users
Comply with legal obligations
We do not use your data for medical, fitness, or health profiling.

4. Sharing of Information
We may share limited information:

With other users, only as necessary to participate in group walks (e.g., name or contact details if you choose to share)
With service providers who help operate the App (e.g., hosting, email services), under confidentiality obligations
When required by law, regulation, or legal process
To protect the rights, safety, or property of users or the App
5. Data Storage and Security
We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute data security.

6. Data Retention
We retain your personal information only for as long as:

Your account remains active, or
It is necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, or
Required by law
You may request deletion of your account and personal data at any time.

7. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

Access your personal data
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Request deletion of your data
Withdraw consent for certain data uses
Object to or restrict processing of your data
Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

8. Third-Party Services
The App may contain links or integrations with third-party services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in the App, and continued use constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
